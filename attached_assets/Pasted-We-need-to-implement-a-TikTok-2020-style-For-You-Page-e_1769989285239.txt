We need to implement a TikTok 2020-style For You Page engine for the Pulse Home Feed only.

This feed is pure discovery and culture.
It must behave like early TikTok FYP, not Instagram, not Reels, not a business feed.

1. CORE PRINCIPLES (NON-NEGOTIABLE)

Rank individual posts, NOT creators or accounts

Followers must NOT influence ranking

Businesses, consumers, photographers, influencers are treated equally

No paid boosting

No profile authority weighting

No conversion or booking bias

Optimize ONLY for attention and retention

Pulse = entertainment + discovery
Pro = business + intent (DO NOT TOUCH PRO)

2. CONTENT ELIGIBILITY (Pulse Only)

Include:

Short-form vertical videos (Reels/TikTok format)

Photos (lower priority than video)

Any user role allowed

Exclude:

Ads

Sponsored posts

Explicitly tagged promotional/business intent

Pro-only content

3. RANKING SIGNALS (TikTok 2020 Weights)

Use these signals ONLY for Pulse ranking:

Primary Signals (Highest Weight)

Average watch time

Completion rate (video watched to end)

Rewatches (same user watches again)

Secondary Signals

Shares

Saves

Comments (quality > quantity)

Low-Weight Signal

Likes (cosmetic only)

Explicitly IGNORE:

Follower count

Account age

Verification

Profile completeness

Location bias (unless used only to seed test pool)

4. COLD START DISTRIBUTION (MANDATORY)

Every new Pulse post must enter a cold-start test pool:

Initial distribution: ~300–1,000 impressions

Majority non-followers

Users selected based on interest similarity, not social graph

Track performance over the first 30–90 minutes.

5. ESCALATION TIERS (REQUIRED)

Implement tier-based distribution:

Tier 1: ~1k impressions

Tier 2: ~10k impressions

Tier 3: ~50k impressions

Tier 4: Unlimited viral distribution

A post advances ONLY if it beats thresholds at each tier.

If it fails at any tier → distribution stops (no penalty to account).

6. INTEREST GRAPH (SIMPLIFIED BUT REQUIRED)

Users should be clustered by behavior, not demographics:

What they watch fully

What they skip instantly

What they rewatch

What they share/save

Pulse feed should match posts to users based on micro-interests, not who they follow.

Interest signals should decay over time (recent behavior matters more).

7. AUDIO & VISUAL SIGNALS

Boost matching when:

Same or similar audio is reused

Visual similarity exists (scene, objects, format)

Hashtags and captions are secondary — use them only for categorization, not ranking.

8. FRONTEND REQUIREMENTS (Pulse Feed UI)

Full-screen vertical scroll (TikTok-style)

One post at a time

Autoplay video

Tap to pause/play

Loop on completion

Infinite scroll

No grids.
No mixed Pro content.
No ads inserted.

9. SAFETY & SUPPRESSION (LIGHT TOUCH)

Do NOT shadowban accounts.

If a post performs poorly:

Reduce distribution

Do not punish future posts

Suppress only if:

Duplicate reposts

Watermarks from other platforms

Extremely low watch time (instant swipes)

10. HARD CONSTRAINTS

This logic applies ONLY to the Pulse Home Feed

Do NOT reuse Pro feed ranking logic

Do NOT add business, booking, or commerce signals

Do NOT privilege verified or business accounts

Do NOT insert ads into Pulse logic

Pulse is sacred discovery.

11. DELIVERABLES EXPECTED

Backend ranking logic for Pulse feed

Clear separation between Pulse and Pro feed pipelines

Documented scoring formula

Clear tier thresholds (can be configurable)

Frontend feed behavior aligned with TikTok UX
