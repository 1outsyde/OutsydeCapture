I need you to build the frontend mobile components for our influencer tracking system inside the Outwyd app. The backend team is building the tracking infrastructure — your job is to handle everything the user and influencer sees and interacts with on the mobile side. Here is a full breakdown:

REFERRAL LINK CAPTURE ON APP OPEN
— When a user opens the app via an influencer's referral link (either from a browser or deep link), the app must capture and store the referral parameter (example: ref=john_smith) immediately on launch
— This parameter must be passed to the backend with every subsequent event: sign-up, first purchase, repeat purchase
— If the app is not yet installed, the referral parameter must survive through: click → app store → install → first open. Work with the backend team on the deep linking service (AppsFlyer, Adjust, or Branch.io) to make sure this handoff works correctly
— If the user already has the app installed, the deep link should open the app directly to the specific product or service page being promoted

INFLUENCER POST WITH EMBEDDED CTA
For posts made directly inside the Outwyd app by an influencer:
— The post must display a clear CTA (Call to Action) button that links directly to the product or service being promoted
— The CTA button must embed the influencer's unique referral parameter in the link so the backend can track who the click came from
— When a user taps the CTA, the app must log a click event and send it to the backend before redirecting the user to the product or service page
— CTA button copy examples: "Shop now", "Book now", "Download here", "Get it here"

INFLUENCER STATS DASHBOARD (IN-APP)
Build an in-app screen for influencers to view their own performance:
— Their unique referral link (with a copy button)
— Total link clicks
— Total app downloads attributed to them
— Total sign-ups attributed to them
— Total purchases attributed to them
— Current point balance
— Total commission earned
— Current tier (Bronze, Silver, Gold, Elite) displayed clearly with a visual indicator
— Progress bar or indicator showing how close they are to the next tier based on their current rolling average conversion rate

ONBOARDING FLOW FOR NEW INFLUENCERS
When a new influencer signs up or is approved on Outwyd:
— Display their unique referral link prominently
— Show clear instructions on where to place the link: bio on Instagram and TikTok, description on YouTube, pinned post on Twitter
— Explain the commission-only structure for their first post and how tiers work
— Show them the tier thresholds: Bronze 0 to 1%, Silver 1 to 3%, Gold 3 to 6%, Elite 6% and above

EXTERNAL PLATFORM CTA GUIDANCE
For influencers promoting on external platforms (Instagram, TikTok, Twitter, YouTube):
— Their unique link should be short and clean (work with backend to provide a shortened version)
— The app should show them their link formatted and ready to copy for each platform
— Display guidance that on Instagram and TikTok they should place the link in their bio and direct viewers there in their caption or video
— On YouTube they can place it directly in the video description
— On Twitter or X they can include it directly in the post

IMPORTANT TECHNICAL NOTES
— All click and conversion events must be sent to the backend in real time, not batched
— The referral parameter must persist through the entire user journey from first click to purchase — do not drop it at any step
— Work closely with the backend team on the deep linking setup to ensure external traffic is attributed correctly
— The stats dashboard must refresh in real time or near real time so influencers can monitor their performance
— All UI must match the existing Outwyd design system
