AVAILABLE BACKEND ENDPOINTS (FINAL)
------------------------

PAYMENT (SERVICE BOOKINGS – IN-APP)

Business appointments:
POST /api/bookings/appointments/:id/create-payment-intent

Photographer shoots:
POST /api/bookings/photographer/:id/create-payment-intent

Response includes:
- clientSecret (for Stripe PaymentSheet)
- paymentIntentId
- capture_method (automatic | manual)

Behavior:
- autoAcceptBookings = true → automatic capture → CONFIRMED
- autoAcceptBookings = false → manual capture → PENDING_PROVIDER (24h)
